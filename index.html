About the Event

Hoops for Hope Charity Game is a community-driven basketball event dedicated to bringing people together for a great cause. Hosted at the legendary Rucker Park in New York, this event will feature exciting pick-up games, special guest appearances, and a chance to support a meaningful charity. Whether you're hitting the court or cheering from the sidelines, this is an opportunity to have fun while giving back. Join us on April 20, 2025, at 5 PM EST to make a difference through the game we all love!

Event Schedule

5v5 Tournament

5v5 tournaments will run from 9:30 am to 1:00 pm. Winning team of the the 5v5 cohort will be announced shortly after!

3v3 pick-up Tournament

3v3 tournaments will run from 3:30 pm to 7:00 pm. Winning team of the the 3v3 cohort will be announced shortly after!

Half-Court Shot Contest and Halftime Performance

At 1:15 PM, three lucky fans from the crowd will have the opportunity to participate in the Half-Court Shot Contest, each taking a shot for a chance to win $1,000. Following the contest, the excitement continues with a special halftime performance featuring a surprise celebrity guest. This exclusive performance will be for both the players and the audience, making it a truly unforgettable moment of the event.
